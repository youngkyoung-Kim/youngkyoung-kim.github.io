About Me

I am a Master's student in Electrical Engineering specializing in ferroelectric field-effect transistors (FeFETs), oxide semiconductor thin-film transistors (IGZO/ITZO), and neuromorphic computing devices.

My research focuses on the design and characterization of MFS/MIFS FeFET structures, memory window engineering, polarization switching mechanisms, and device reliability (retention, endurance, and variability).
